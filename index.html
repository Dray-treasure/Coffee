About Us

At Cafe Garden, coffee isn’t just a drink — it’s an experience. Our journey began in a tiny kitchen where the aroma of freshly ground beans filled the air and friends gathered to share stories over warm mugs. Today, we’ve grown into your cozy neighborhood café, but our heart remains the same: to create a space where everyone feels welcome, whether you’re here for a quick morning latte, a slow afternoon pastry, or an evening catch-up with loved ones. We source our beans from sustainable farms around the world, bake our pastries fresh each morning, and prepare every drink with care — because we believe good coffee brings people together. And in our corner of the world, every cup tells a story.
